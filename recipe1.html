Spaghetti Bolognese

Ingredients:

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 400g canned crushed tomatoes
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Grated Parmesan cheese (optional)

Preparation👨‍🍳:

  1. Cook the spaghetti in boiling salted water according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a pan, heat olive oil. Sauté garlic and onion until soft.
  3. Add crushed tomatoes, oregano, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 10–15 minutes.
  4. Add the cooked spaghetti and toss to coat with the sauce.
  5. Serve with grated cheese if desired.
