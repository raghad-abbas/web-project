Shawarma

Ingredients:

  • 500g chicken breast, thinly sliced
  • 2 tablespoons yogurt
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon cumin
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • Salt and pepper
  • Pita bread and garlic sauce for serving

  1. In a bowl, mix yogurt, lemon juice, oil, and spices.
  2. Add the chicken, coat well, and marinate for at least 1 hour.
  3. Cook in a hot pan until golden and cooked through.
  4. Serve in pita bread with garlic sauce and vegetables.
