Baked Sweet Potato 🍠

Ingredients:

  • 2 medium sweet potatoes
  • 1 tbsp olive oil (optional)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Optional toppings: Greek yogurt, chopped herbs, feta cheese, black beans

Preparation👨‍🍳:

  1. Prep the potatoes: Wash and scrub sweet potatoes well. Pat dry. Pierce each a few times with a fork.
  2. Bake: Place on a baking tray lined with foil. Bake in a preheated oven at 200°C (400°F) for 45–60 minutes, until soft inside.
  3. Serve: Slice open, season with salt and pepper. Add toppings of choice (healthy options like yogurt or black beans work great).
