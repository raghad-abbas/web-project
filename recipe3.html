Cheesy Pizza

Ingredients:

  • 1 ready-made pizza dough
  • 1/2 cup tomato sauce
  • 1 & 1/2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 teaspoon oregano
  • Olive oil for brushing

Preparation👨‍🍳:

  1. Preheat the oven to 220°C (428°F).
  2. Roll out the pizza dough on a baking sheet.
  3. Spread the tomato sauce evenly on the dough.
  4. Sprinkle mozzarella cheese on top, then add oregano.
  5. Brush the edges with olive oil.
  6. Bake for 12-15 minutes or until the crust is golden and the cheese is melted.
  7. Slice and serve
