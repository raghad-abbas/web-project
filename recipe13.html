Brownies

Ingredients:

  • ½ cup butter (melted)
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • ⅓ cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • ½ cup all-purpose flour
  • ¼ tsp salt
  • ¼ tsp baking powder
  • Optional: chocolate chips or walnuts

Preparation👨‍🍳 :

  1. Mix wet ingredients: In a bowl, mix melted butter, sugar, eggs, and vanilla.
  2. Add dry ingredients: Stir in cocoa, flour, salt, and baking powder.
  3. Add extras: Fold in chocolate chips or nuts if using.
  4. Bake: Pour into a greased square baking pan. Bake at 175°C (350°F) for 20–25 minutes.
  5. Cool and serve: Let cool before slicing.
