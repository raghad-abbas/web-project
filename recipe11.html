Donuts 🍩

Ingredients:

  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup warm milk
  • 2¼ tsp active dry yeast
  • ¼ cup sugar
  • ¼ cup melted butter
  • 1 egg
  • ½ tsp salt
  • Oil for frying
  • Optional: chocolate glaze, powdered sugar, sprinkles

Preparation👨‍🍳:

  1. Activate yeast: Mix warm milk, sugar, and yeast. Let it sit 5-10 minutes until foamy.
  2. Make dough: Add egg, melted butter, and salt to yeast mix. Gradually add flour and knead until smooth and soft (about 10 minutes).
  3. Let it rise: Cover and let it rise in a warm place for 1 hour or until doubled.
  4. Shape: Roll out the dough to 1 cm thickness. Use a round cutter (and smaller one for center hole). Let rest 15 minutes.
  5. Fry: Heat oil in a deep pan. Fry donuts until golden on both sides. Drain on paper towels.
  6. Decorate: Dip in melted chocolate or glaze, add sprinkles or powdered sugar.
