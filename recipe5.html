Beef stroganoff

Ingredients:

  • 400g beef strips
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 1 cup mushrooms, sliced
  • 1 tablespoon flour
  • 1/2 cup beef broth
  • 1/2 cup sour cream
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • Salt and pepper to taste
