Kunafa

Ingredients:

  • 500g kunafa dough (shredded phyllo)
  • 250g melted butter
  • 2 cups sweet cheese (akkawi, mozzarella, or ricotta)
  • 1 cup sugar syrup (see below)
  • Optional: pistachios for garnish

Preparation👨‍🍳:

  1. Prepare syrup: Boil sugar and water for 8 minutes. Add lemon juice. Add rose water at the end. Let cool.
  2. Prepare kunafa base: Mix shredded kunafa with melted butter well.
  3. Assemble: In a greased round tray, add half of kunafa and press down. Add cheese filling, then cover with remaining kunafa.
  4. Bake: Bake at 180°C (350°F) for 30-40 minutes until golden.
  5. Finish: Flip onto a plate, pour syrup on top, and garnish with pistachios. Serve warm.
