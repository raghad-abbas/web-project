Zinger Burger

Ingredients:

  • 2 chicken breasts
  • 1 cup buttermilk
  • 1 cup flour
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • Salt and pepper
  • Lettuce, pickles, and burger buns
  • Mayonnaise or spicy sauce

Preparation👨‍🍳:

  1. Marinate chicken in buttermilk for at least 1 hour.
  2. Mix flour with spices in a bowl.
  3. Dredge chicken in flour mixture, then deep-fry until crispy and golden.
  4. Toast buns, spread mayonnaise, and layer lettuce, pickles, and chicken.
  5. Serve hot.
