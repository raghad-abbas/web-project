Fresh Salad

Ingredients:

  • 1 cucumber, diced
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1 carrot, grated
  • Lettuce leaves, chopped
  • Juice of 1 lemon
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

  1. Wash and chop all vegetables.
  2. In a large bowl, combine cucumber, tomato, onion, carrot, and lettuce.
  3. In a small bowl, whisk together lemon juice, olive oil, salt, and pepper.
  4. Pour the dressing over the salad and toss well.
  5. Serve immediately.
