Sayadieh

Ingredients:

  • 1 kg white fish (like seabass or cod)
  • 2 cups rice
  • 2 large onions (sliced)
  • 4 cups water or fish stock
  • 2 tsp cumin
  • 1 tsp cinnamon
  • Salt & pepper to taste
  • Oil for frying
  • Roasted nuts (for garnish)
  • Lemon wedges (for serving)

  1. Clean the fish and season with salt and pepper.
  2. Fry fish until golden. Set aside.
  3. In a pot, fry onions until deep brown. Remove half for garnish.
  4. Add spices to the pot with the remaining onions.
  5. Add rice and stir for 2 minutes.
  6. Add water or fish stock. Bring to a boil.
  7. Lower heat, cover, and simmer until rice is cooked.
  8. Serve rice topped with fish and caramelized onions. Garnish with nuts and lemon.
