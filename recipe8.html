Mansaf

Ingredients:

  • 1 kg lamb (cut into medium pieces)
  • 2 cups jameed (dried yogurt) or 1 liter liquid jameed or laban
  • 2 cups rice (washed and soaked)
  • 1 large onion (quartered)
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 1 tsp cardamom pods
  • ¬Ω tsp turmeric (for color)
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tbsp ghee or butter
  • Almonds and pine nuts (roasted)
  • Shrak or markook bread (optional)

    • If liquid, just heat it up gently.
  3. Add cooked meat to the jameed sauce and simmer for 20 minutes.
  4. Cook rice with ghee and turmeric until fluffy.

  6. Pour hot jameed sauce generously over the top.
