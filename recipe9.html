Shakshuka

Ingredients:

  • 4 eggs
  • 1 onion (finely chopped)
  • 2 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 1 red bell pepper (chopped)
  • 4 medium tomatoes (chopped) or 1 cup canned tomato
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • ½ tsp cumin
  • Salt & black pepper to taste
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • Fresh parsley for garnish (optional)

Preparation👨‍🍳:

  1. In a skillet, heat olive oil. Sauté onion until soft.
  2. Add garlic and bell pepper. Cook until slightly tender.
  3. Add tomatoes and spices. Simmer for 10–15 minutes until thickened.
  4. Make small wells and crack eggs into them.
  5. Cover and cook until eggs are just set (soft or firm as preferred).
  6. Garnish with parsley. Serve with bread.
